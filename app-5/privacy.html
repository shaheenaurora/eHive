Privacy Policy

Effective at launch — 1 August 2026. This policy governs ehive.ae and the eHive member portal.

What we collect

Only what you hand us directly: your name, email, phone number and the answers you give our intake forms (business activity, product or tier interest, questions). If you book a call, we store the slot you chose so the right person shows up.

What we use it for

  • Routing your request to the right team and replying to it
  • Sending what you explicitly asked for — a quote, a breakdown, the Journal
  • Operating your membership, if you join the Circle

We do not sell your data. We do not buy lists. We do not email you about things you didn’t ask for.

What we never do

No advertising trackers, no third-party data brokers, no shadow profiles. Analytics, where used, are aggregate and privacy-respecting.

Full policy at launch

The complete policy — retention periods, processor list, your rights under UAE law and how to exercise them — is published here before the first form goes live. Until then, this page states our practice in plain language, and we honor it.
