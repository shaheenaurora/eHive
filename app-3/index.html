The eHive belief

The process

How it works

  1. 1

    Tell us where you are.

    A short intake — starting up, running and needing clarity, or looking to join a community of peers.

  2. 2

    We match you to the right door.

    Business Setup for formation and licensing. Consulting for a specific sprint. Circle for belonging and network.

  3. 3

    You book directly.

    Real dates, real pricing where possible, no back-and-forth for standard engagements.

  4. 4

    You keep going.

    Most founders end up using more than one pillar over time — that’s the design, not an upsell.
